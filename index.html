About ECO-OLIVA Manufacturing

Eco-Oliva Manufacturing is a startup founded in Kairouan, in October 2020. It was established in a rural area surrounded by thousands of olive trees, which makes the supply of olive leaves easy and quick.

Welcome to Eco-Oliva

The aim of this Startup is the production of whole dried olive leaves, fine cut olive leaves and olive leaves Powder, which will be used in the Para-pharmaceutical, cosmetic and Tea Industries.
